THE COMPANY

Linbuch Global Resources Ltd. (LGR) is a privately owned company, incorporated in 2023 under the Companies and Allied Matters Act of Nigeria 2020. The company is a leading industrial company for procurement and supply of petroleum products and industrial equipment.

We focus on providing diverse services to the downstream and upstream sectors of the Oil and Gas Industry and various sectors of the Nigerian economy with our services, ranging from Engineering, Construction, Procurement and Logistics to general service and maintenance, among others.
