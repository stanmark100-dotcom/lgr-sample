FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

FAQs

What services do you offer?

We offer integrated solutions including strategic procurement services, general facility services (such as cleaning and utilities management), and comprehensive maintenance (both preventative and reactive) for commercial properties.

What types of clients do you work with?

We work with various commercial clients, including office buildings, industrial sites, multi-tenant properties, and government entities, adapting our services to the specific needs and size of each facility.

How do you differ from other service providers?

Our key differentiator is our integrated approach, providing a single point of contact for procurement and all facility maintenance needs, which streamlines operations and ensures consistent quality and accountability.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed to operate and hold all relevant certifications and insurances, including public liability and workers' compensation, to ensure compliance and peace of mind.

What is your procurement process?

Our process involves initial spend analysis and opportunity assessment to identify savings, followed by strategic sourcing, vendor evaluation, contract negotiation, and ongoing supplier relationship management.

How do you approach preventive maintenance?

Our approach to preventive maintenance involves regular inspections, scheduled servicing, and proactive replacement of components to minimize downtime and extend asset life.

What safety measures do you take on site?

Safety is non-negotiable. Our workers undergo regular, documented safety training, follow strict internal safety protocols, and comply with all state and national Work Health and Safety (WHS) laws.
